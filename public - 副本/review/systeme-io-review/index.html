What Is Systeme.io?

Systeme.io is a French-born all-in-one online business platform founded by Aurelien Amacker. It combines sales funnel building, email marketing, website creation, online course hosting, affiliate management, CRM pipelines, booking calendars, community building, evergreen webinars, and physical product sales – all under one roof.

Over 500,000 entrepreneurs use it globally as of 2026. The platform’s boldest promise? You can run most of your online business without paying for a separate tool ever again.

Feature Deep Dive

1. Sales Funnels

The funnel builder is Systeme.io’s flagship feature. It includes:

  • Drag-and-drop editor – no coding, works intuitively
  • Pre-built templates – opt-in funnels, sales funnels, webinar funnels, bridge funnels
  • One-click upsells – classic ClickFunnels-style post-purchase offers
  • Order bumps – add complementary products at checkout
  • Countdown timers – create urgency without third-party scripts
  • A/B split testing – test headlines, CTAs, and page designs
  • Unlimited funnel steps on paid plans

Bottom line: Good enough for 95% of marketers. If you’ve used ClickFunnels before, you’ll feel right at home – minus the $297/mo bill. Try Systeme.io free

2. Email Marketing

Systeme.io’s email system is fully integrated with everything else:

  • Unlimited email sends on every plan (yes, even free)
  • Broadcast campaigns – send to segments or full list
  • Automated sequences – trigger-based multi-step flows
  • Tagging & segmentation – organize contacts by behavior
  • Email templates – basic but clean
  • SMS marketing – add text messages to automations

3. Online Courses

The course builder is surprisingly capable for an all-in-one tool:

  • Video lessons – upload or embed from YouTube/Vimeo
  • Drip content scheduling – release lessons on a timer
  • PDF downloads – attach resources to any lesson
  • Module locking – prerequisites before advancing
  • Student progress tracking – see who’s completing what
  • Auto-enrollment – students get enrolled automatically on purchase
  • Quizzes & assignments – basic but functional

What’s missing: No built-in live classes, no Zoom integration beyond calendar, and no advanced gamification (badges, leaderboards).

4. Website Builder

Simple – get a multi-page site live in minutes:

  • Pre-designed templates – niche-specific layouts
  • Custom domain – connect your own domain
  • Blog – built-in blogging engine with categories
  • Responsive by default – looks good on mobile
  • SEO settings – meta titles, descriptions, alt tags

This won’t replace a fully custom Webflow or WordPress site, but for a landing page + blog combo, it’s more than adequate.

5. Affiliate Management

A standout feature – you can run a full affiliate program from day one:

  • Custom commission rates – percentage or flat fee
  • Affiliate links – auto-generated for each affiliate
  • Real-time tracking – see clicks, conversions, commissions
  • Automated payouts – via PayPal or manual
  • Affiliate dashboard – your affiliates get their own login
  • Free on every plan

Most competitors charge extra for this or limit it to premium tiers. The Systeme.io affiliate program is one of the most accessible for new users.

6. Evergreen Webinars

Run automated, on-demand webinars that feel live:

  • Pre-recorded video – plays on a schedule
  • Automated replays – multiple show times
  • Call-to-action integration – sell during or after the webinar
  • Email follow-ups – triggered based on attendance

Competitors like EverWebinar charge $99/mo for this alone.

7. Booking Calendar

A Calendly alternative built in:

  • Custom availability – set your hours
  • Automated confirmations – email triggers on booking
  • Payment collection – take deposits or full payment at booking
  • Zoom/Google Meet integration – auto-generate meeting links

8. Communities

Built-in community spaces:

  • Paid or free – gate access to your community
  • Posts & comments – member discussion
  • Connected to courses – perfect for cohort-based programs
  • Member management – approve, remove, or assign roles

9. CRM Pipelines

A simple Kanban-style pipeline manager:

  • Custom deal stages – create your sales process
  • Drag-and-drop deals – move contacts through stages
  • Automated follow-ups – trigger emails on stage changes
  • Connected to funnels – new leads appear automatically

The Systeme.io CRM is lightweight compared to GoHighLevel or HubSpot, but for solopreneurs tracking a handful of active deals, it gets the job done without a separate tool.

10. Sub-accounts

For agencies and multi-brand managers:

  • Fully isolated workspaces – one per client/brand
  • Separate funnels, contacts, emails, and courses – no cross-contamination
  • Single dashboard – switch between accounts without logging out

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Systeme.io legit?

Yes. Systeme.io is a legitimate company founded in 2018, headquartered in Bordeaux, France. Over 500,000 users trust it, and it processes millions of dollars in transactions monthly. It’s not a fly-by-night tool.

Can I really use Systeme.io for free?

Yes. The Free plan gives you 2,000 contacts, unlimited emails, one sales funnel (up to 3 steps), one course (up to 3 active students), a blog, and affiliate management – all at $0, no credit card required.

Does Systeme.io handle payment processing?

Systeme.io integrates with Stripe, PayPal, Razorpay, Flutterwave, Mercado Pago, and Xendit. It does not process payments natively – you’ll need one of these gateways.

How does Systeme.io compare to ClickFunnels 2.0?

ClickFunnels 2.0 has more polished funnel templates, a larger community, and deeper analytics. Systeme.io matches most core features at 1/5 the price and adds courses, communities, and booking that ClickFunnels lacks.

Can I sell physical products on Systeme.io?

Yes. You can add physical products to your order forms with variants (size, color), inventory tracking, shipping fee calculation, and order bumps. It’s basic but functional.

What languages does Systeme.io support?

The platform is available in English, French, Spanish, Portuguese, German, Italian, Dutch, Polish, and more. Higher plans allow multi-language websites.

Does Systeme.io offer a mobile app?

Yes, but it is student-centric. The admin dashboard management is limited on mobile. Students can access courses and content, but as an account owner you’ll find most admin features work better on desktop.

What’s the best Systeme.io plan for a course creator?

The Startup plan ($27/mo) is enough for most course creators starting out. Go for Unlimited ($97/mo) when you have over 10k students and need 0% transaction fees + unlimited courses.

Is Systeme.io good for beginners?

Absolutely. The onboarding wizard guides you through setting up your first funnel, email, and product. The free plan means you can learn without financial pressure. It’s one of the most beginner-friendly all-in-one platforms available.

How does Systeme.io pricing compare to alternatives?

Systeme.io’s pricing undercuts every major competitor: ClickFunnels ($147/mo), Kajabi ($149/mo), Kartra ($119/mo), and GoHighLevel ($97/mo). The Free plan has no time limit and is not a trial.

Final Verdict

After testing Systeme.io alongside ClickFunnels, Kajabi, and MailerLite, we found it delivers one of the strongest value propositions for solo creators – particularly if you’re looking for an all-in-one platform with a generous free plan.

If you’re launching your first online business or replacing multiple subscriptions, Systeme.io is one of the easiest platforms to recommend in 2026. The free plan is sufficient to explore its core workflow before deciding whether a paid plan is worthwhile.

It’s not perfect. Email design is basic, customer support is slow on lower tiers, and heavy customization isn’t its strength. But for 90% of online business use cases, it’s more than enough – at a price that leaves room for everything else you need to spend on (ads, tools, outsourcing).

Who should buy it:

  • Solopreneurs who want one dashboard, one bill
  • Budget-conscious course creators
  • Anyone tired of paying for separate tools
  • Beginners looking for a free all-in-one marketing platform to learn on

Who should skip it:

  • Large teams needing collaboration features
