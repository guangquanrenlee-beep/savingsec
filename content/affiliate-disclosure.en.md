## Affiliate Disclosure

SavingSec participates in various e-commerce platform affiliate programs.

### What is Affiliate Marketing?

Affiliate marketing is a performance-based advertising model. When we direct users to our partner merchants' websites through links and a purchase is made, we may earn a commission.

### Does this affect your purchase price?

**No.** The price of products purchased through our links is exactly the same as visiting the merchant's website directly.

### How do we ensure fairness?

- Review content is based on actual usage experience
- Whether we recommend or not has nothing to do with commission levels
- We clearly label all affiliate marketing links
- We prioritize recommending products with real value
