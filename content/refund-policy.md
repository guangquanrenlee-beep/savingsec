## Refund & Support Guide

### Important Information

**SavingSec is a shopping guide website that does not directly sell products and assumes no responsibility for product after-sales service.**

### After-Sales Service Processing

All after-sales services related to products, including refunds, returns, exchanges, and warranties, are handled by the e-commerce platform or merchant that sold the product. All refunds are processed exclusively through the respective e-commerce platform, not through SavingSec.

### Common Issues Guide

**1. Coupons Cannot Be Used**
- Please check if the coupon is still valid
- Confirm if the terms of use are met (minimum order value, applicable products, etc.)
- If the coupon is expired or cannot be used, please search for current deals on the corresponding e-commerce platform

**2. Order-Related Issues**
- Order status check: Please log in to the corresponding e-commerce platform to check
- Tracking: Use the tracking number provided by the e-commerce platform
- Shipping delays: Please contact the merchant's customer service directly

**3. Refunds and Returns**
- Refund application: Submit your request through the order page on the e-commerce platform
- Return process: Follow the instructions from the e-commerce platform
- Refund timeline: Subject to each e-commerce platform's refund policy
- **Note:** All refunds are processed through the e-commerce platform, not through SavingSec

**4. Product Quality Issues**
- Please inspect the product as soon as possible after receipt
- If there are quality issues, please request after-sales service through the e-commerce platform
- Keep the product packaging and relevant receipts

**5. Warranty Service**
- Product warranty is the responsibility of the brand or merchant
- Please keep your purchase receipt and warranty card
- Contact the brand's official customer service or authorized repair centers
