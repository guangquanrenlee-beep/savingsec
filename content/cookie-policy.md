## Cookie Policy

The SavingSec website uses cookies and similar technologies to provide, protect, and improve our services.

### What are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored in your browser. They are commonly used to make websites function effectively.

### Types of Cookies We Use

**1. Essential Cookies**
These cookies are necessary for the proper operation of the website. They ensure basic functionality such as page navigation.

**2. Analytics Cookies**
We use analytics cookies to understand how users interact with our website and to improve content and user experience.

**3. Functional Cookies**
These cookies allow the website to remember your choices (such as language settings) and provide a more personalized experience.

### Third-Party Cookies

Our website may contain third-party content, such as social media plugins, embedded videos, or advertisements. These third parties may set their own cookies. We have no control over these cookies and recommend you review the respective third-party privacy policies.

### GDPR Notice (EU Users)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), we obtain your consent to use non-essential cookies. You can withdraw your consent at any time through the cookie settings on our website.

### Managing Cookies

You can manage or delete cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling cookies may affect certain website features.

Most browsers allow you to:
- View all cookies and delete them individually
- Block third-party cookies
- Block all cookies
- Delete all cookies when you close your browser
