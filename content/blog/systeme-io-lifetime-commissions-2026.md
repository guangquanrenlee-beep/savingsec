### Workaround 2: Build a Manual Attribution Spreadsheet to Compensate for the Dashboard

Since the dashboard doesn't tell you which content drove which conversion, set up a manual tracking system:

1. Create a unique campaign name for each content piece (e.g., `blog-clickfunnels-alternative`, `yt-free-funnel-tutorial`, `email-november-promo`)

This is a 15-20 minute setup with basic PHP or a WordPress plugin like Pretty Links. It's not as good as native attribution, but it's functional enough to tell you whether your YouTube channel or your blog is the better channel to double down on.
